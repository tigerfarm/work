Stacy David is a traveling computer instructor. His writing and teaching skills have taken him many times around the world. Paris was one of his stops while returning from a tour of work in India. He had stopped in London for a day, then traveled by Eurostar, the high speed train, under the English Channel, through the Chunnel, to Paris to see the Louvre and visit his uncle.
