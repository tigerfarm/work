Before Google Maps was popular, before smartphones, before Google Maps covered Asian cities, I wrote this map program to inspire students to be as good as Google's California developers.

This was part of a course I taught in Atlanta, Singapore, Kula Lumpur, Bangkok, and Hyderabad.
